🚀 NeighbourGuard™ News Ticker Demo

This demo showcases the AfricaTalking-inspired news ticker component that fetches real-time news from the NeighbourGuard™ platform. The ticker automatically loads news from the /general/serviceInfo/news endpoint and displays them with smooth scrolling animations.

🔧 Technical Specifications

  • Endpoint: GET /general/serviceInfo/news
  • Firebase Path: /services/neighbourGuardService/news
  • Auto Refresh: Every 5 minutes
  • Scroll Speed: Configurable (default 50 seconds)
  • Priority Support: High, normal, low with visual indicators
  • Category Badges: Color-coded news categorization
